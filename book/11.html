4 Applications

In our research we are working with rapid prototyping approaches and empirical evaluations of the underlying concepts and our implementation. In the following you can learn about some prototypes and software frameworks developed in the Mobile Media group in CELSTEC.

  • Mobile Augmented Reality: we have developed a simple app for playing mobile augmented reality games in groups.
  • Field trip support system: we have a field trip tool suite supporting teachers in preparing field trips, students during the field trip for answering questions and annotating real world environments, as also a tool for teacher to monitor the field trip process in real time.
  • We have build solutions for multi-interface access to LMS supporting cross context learning situations.
  • We have build situated learning support tools as context-blogger which is used to annotate real world objects and share images and discussion with peers in real time.
  • We have build real time awareness systems for groups aggregating information from existing personal information tools and providing smartphone and ambient display access to group information.
  • We have created workflow and content management tools for mobile content creation and management. This is at the core for Open Universiteits offers on iTunes U and Youtube EDU.
