5.5 Ambient Displays

Displays have become a pervasive part of our environment. While they were traditionally used for advertising purposes researchers have started in the last years to exploit their potential for learning purposes, to research cognitive effects and to exploit them for interactions of users with their environment. Ambient learning displays are an emerging interdisciplinary topic to which different disciplines contribute.

Research questions related to our work on ambient displays are:

  • Which types of digital contents can support learning and reflection in ubiquitous learning environments ?
  • Which sensors can be used and how must they be aggregated, filtered, and implemented in ubiquitous learning environments?
  • Which methods of visualization, and audification can be used to create awareness in ubiquitous learning environments?
  • How are the awareness methods assimilated and perceived in ubiquitous learning environments and what are the implications for the design?
  • Does the utilization of contextualized digital contents support and enhance learning and reflection in ubiquitous learning environments and what are the effects?
